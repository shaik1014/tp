TRENDING

JAWAN:

Driven by a desire to rectify societal wrongs, Vikram embarks on a mission to obliterate Kalee and presents the government with a set of demands, setting the stage for an electrifying and high-stakes showdown. The film delivers a strong social message about issues like the corrupt political system, challenges faced by farmers, and flaws in the healthcare system. It emphasizes the power of the index finger during elections as a means for citizens to actively participate in shaping the destiny of their country.
